About

Our mission

It is clear that during this period of confinement, the number of online players on all platforms combined increased significantly. "Stay at home" is the watchword, so it's normal to find other daily occupations. Combining business with pleasure, our mission is to provide additional help to the front lines affected by COVID19 through a FIFA20 competition.

Let's support them!

On the front line, hospitals are poised for a long crisis and facing a shortage of breathing machines. The CHU Saint Pierre hospital has already called for donations to benefit from new equipment given the lack of budget at present.

As a mark of support for the entire medical profession, we will donate all of the registration fees collected to CHU Saint Pierre.

This simple little gesture may not mean much to you, but bringing our stone together can make an impact whatever it is.

QUALIFICATIONS
 01 - 02 April 2020

Registered players will be invited to start the group phase comprising at least 3 games over a period of 2 days. The first two of each hen will move on to the next phase.

FINAL TABLE
 03 april 2020

Qualified players will be randomly assigned to the final table. The matches will include extra time and penalties this time given the “direct elimination” nature of this phase.
